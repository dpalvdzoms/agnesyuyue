余 钺

北京大学中文系，理论语言学

我的名字是余钺，国际音标为/y35 yɛ51/。“钺”在古代中国是一种斧形兵器，也是象征着君王权力的礼器。你也可以使用我的藏文名 དཔལ་འཛོམས། /hua.ndzom/（音近“花粽”）或英文名 Agnes 来称呼我。

My name is Yu Yue (pronounced /y35 yɛ51/). In Chinese, the Yue is an axe-shaped ancient weapon and a ceremonial symbol of royal authority. You are also welcome to call me by my Tibetan name, དཔལ་འཛོམས། (/hua.ndzom/), or my English name, Agnes.

我于2003年冬天出生于鄂东南的一个小县城，母语为赣语大冶方言和汉语普通话，第一外语为英语，曾短暂学习过安多藏语。

I was born in the winter of 2003 in a small county town in southeastern Hubei province. I am a native speaker of the Daye Gan Chinese and Mandarin, and English is my first foreign language. I have also spent some time briefly learning Amdo Tibetan.

目前的研究兴趣为音系学、历史语言学、语音变异。主要调查赣语大冶方言与云南哈尼语。

Currently, my research interests focus on phonology, historical linguistics, and sound change. My research primarily investigates the Daye Gan Chinese and the Hani in Yunnan.
